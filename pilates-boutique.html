Logo

The new logo needed to have a more professional look, as the firt one I made her was, well, let's just say it needed a more professional look. So a new typeface was chosen and the flower, which was a part of the original logo, was kept to keep brand consitency.

Web

The Pilates Boutique website needed a responsive redesign, and the content needed better hierarchy.

Yes, But Where's The Pricing!

The goal of the redesign was to allow visitors easier access the content they were looking for. Whether that was session pricing packages, or contact info, the new site needed to allow useres to get to what they wanted quickly, and not have it be burried in drop-down menus.

Integrating Vine - 2 Reasons

Michelle, my wife and the owner of Pilates Boutique, also began using Vine as a way to give clients access to simple pilates moves on their own. She calls it Morning Pilates, and each video is a quick 6 second repetative clip of an excercise someone can do on their own. The goal is to provide added value to current customers, while giving potential customers a glimpse into her teaching. The quick sessions have worked to get potential customers to reach out and purchase pilates sessions.
