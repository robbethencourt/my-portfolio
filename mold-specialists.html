mold specialists

mold removal and cleaning company

created / 2011

Logo

The logo was made to look more modern, and be able to work on either light or dark background. This would be ideal for print related coupons and promotions, which is a part of their advertising.

Web

The wbsite needed a redesign in regards to the look and the content. A lot of outdated information was removed, what remained was better organized and accessible from the main menu.

I Could Have Sworn There Was A Link To…

Much of the information on the Mold Specialists website had become outdated, so a new look and content refresh was in order. The layout of the content, and its accessability also needed to be addressed as there were pages on the previous site that could not be accessed from the main menu. Providing an easily accessible menu to all content on the site was a top priority.

Keep It Consistent, Keep It Clean

The look of the site was also completely redone. The owners wanted a cleaner look to the site with bright colors. They clean up mold as their business, so they wanted to convey a clean presence on the web to match what they do. A predominatly light color pallet with white, blues and light grays was chosen.
